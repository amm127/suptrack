Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Information We Collect

SupTrack collects information you provide when creating an account (name, email, password) and data you enter while using the platform, including supervisee records, session notes, hour logs, documents, and payment records.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the SupTrack supervision management platform. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS) with row-level security policies ensuring each supervisor can only access their own data. All data is encrypted in transit via TLS and at rest.

4. HIPAA Considerations

SupTrack is designed for clinical supervision documentation. While we implement security best practices, supervisors are responsible for ensuring their use of the platform complies with applicable privacy regulations. Do not enter protected health information (PHI) of clients into session notes. Session notes should document the supervision interaction, not client-identifying details.

5. AI Features

When you use AI-powered features (session note generation, consultation), your input is sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Anthropic does not use API inputs for model training. No data is retained by the AI provider after processing.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting support. Upon deletion, all supervisee records, session notes, and documents are permanently removed.

7. Cookies

SupTrack uses essential cookies and local storage for authentication and user preferences. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes via email or in-app notification.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at privacy@suptrack.io.
