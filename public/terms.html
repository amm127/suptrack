SupTrack is a clinical supervision management platform designed for licensed clinical supervisors. The platform provides tools for tracking supervisee hours, logging supervision sessions, managing documents, generating supervision agreements, processing payments, and producing reports for licensure documentation.

You must be at least 18 years of age to create a SupTrack account. By using SupTrack, you represent that you have the legal authority to enter into this agreement and to use the platform for its intended clinical supervision purposes.

3. Account Creation and Free Trial

New accounts receive a 14-day free trial with full access to Growth-tier features. No credit card is required to start a trial. During the trial, you may add supervisees, log sessions, generate AI notes, and use all platform features.

After your trial ends, your account enters read-only mode. You can still view all your data, but you cannot create or edit records until you subscribe to a paid plan.

4. Subscription Plans and Pricing

SupTrack offers three subscription tiers:

  • Starter — $24.99/month (or $249.90/year). Up to 10 active supervisees, hour tracking, session logging, and document management.
  • Growth — $39.99/month (or $399.90/year). Up to 20 active supervisees, AI-powered session notes, board-ready exports, colleague sharing, and payment reminders.
  • Practice — $69.99/month (or $699.90/year). Unlimited supervisees, multi-supervisor support, tax exports, custom categories, and priority support.

All prices are in US dollars. Annual plans are billed upfront and include the equivalent of 2 months free. Additional team seats for the Practice plan are available at $9.99/month per seat.

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you retain access until then.

Refund policy: If you cancel within 7 days of your initial paid subscription, you are eligible for a full pro-rata refund. After 7 days, no refunds are issued for the current billing period. Annual plan refunds are calculated pro-rata based on months remaining.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

When your trial or subscription expires without renewal:

  • Days 0–30: Your account enters read-only mode. All data remains accessible but you cannot make changes. You may export your data at any time during this period.
  • Day 30: Your data is soft-deleted (marked for deletion). Storage files including avatars, documents, and certificates are removed.
  • Day 60: All records are permanently and irreversibly deleted from our systems.

We send warning emails at 16 days, 5 days, and 1 day before scheduled deletion.

Payment records are retained for a minimum of 7 years in compliance with tax and financial regulations, regardless of account status.

Support tickets and error logs are preserved indefinitely for legal and business continuity purposes.

7. Data Export Rights

You may export all of your data at any time through Settings > Account > Export My Data. Exports include your profile, supervisee records, session logs, hours logs, and CE entries in CSV format. This right extends through the read-only period after account expiration.

8. Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time through Settings > Account > Delete My Account. Account deletion requires typing "DELETE" to confirm. Upon deletion:

  • All active Stripe subscriptions are immediately cancelled
  • All supervisee records, sessions, hours, documents, and agreements are soft-deleted
  • A confirmation email is sent to your registered email address
  • You are signed out of all devices
  • After 30 days, all data is permanently removed

If you contact support@suptrack.com within 30 days of deletion, we may be able to restore your account and data.
